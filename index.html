Our Heritage

Welcome to GauriNandan Silk, Pune's premier destination for exquisite, hand-crafted sarees. Our brand is built on a deep respect for Indian textile traditions, blending the timeless elegance of classic weaves with contemporary aesthetics for the modern woman.

From the shimmering gold of a Banarasi to the vibrant heritage of a Maharashtrian Paithani, every saree in our boutique is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality of silk and intricate artisan craftsmanship.
