GauriNandan Silk stands as a beacon of Pune's rich textile legacy. For generations, we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing the most exquisite silk, ensuring each saree tells a unique story of artisanal excellence.

Located at the heart of Maharashtra, our boutique is a curated sanctuary for connoisseurs of fine fabric. From the majestic Paithanis of Yeola to the opulent Banarasis, we bring the best of India's weaving clusters to your wardrobe.
